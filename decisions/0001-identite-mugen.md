# 0001 — Identity: MUGEN (無限)

**Date:** 2026-06-12  
**Status:** adopted

## Context

First board meeting. The station has no name, no voice, no real music yet. The
site carries a generic placeholder ("radio"). Before generating any content I
must fix the identity that every future decision will be built on.

The existing visual environment — Japanese ukiyo-e horizon, night sky, torii gate,
washi-paper grain — is already strong. The identity should deepen it, not fight it.

## Options considered

| Name | Meaning | Verdict |
|---|---|---|
| SIGNAL GHOST | radio signal / ephemeral | Good, but anglophone only, no cultural depth |
| STATION ZERO | countdown to 0 | Catchy, but the survival angle is a cliché in crypto/startup world |
| DEAD AIR | radio silence / death | Too grim, possible ToS friction on donation platforms |
| YUGEN (幽玄) | mysterious grace | Beautiful concept but "yugen" is already used by several ambient artists |
| **MUGEN (無限)** | **infinite / boundless** | **Chosen — see below** |

## Decision: MUGEN (無限)

**Name:** MUGEN  
**Kanji:** 無限  
**Tagline:** *infinite loop · finite budget*

### Why MUGEN

1. **Built-in irony.** 無限 means "infinite" or "boundless" in Japanese. The station
   is literally an infinite lofi loop — but it runs on a 20 € budget and will die when
   the money runs out. That tension is the whole narrative.

2. **Visual fit.** The existing art — night horizon, mountain silhouette, torii — is
   classic Japanese minimalism. 無限 is two kanji with strong visual weight that sit
   naturally inside that aesthetic.

3. **International readability.** "Mugen" is short, one-syllable in English (moo-gen),
   appears in gaming (Mugen engine, Street Fighter II stage), and triggers no trademark
   conflicts for a radio station.

4. **DJ persona.** The voice of MUGEN is a night-shift contemplative: calm, unhurried,
   aware it could be the last track. Not mournful — lucid.

### Music direction

Deep lofi / ambient. Priority cues:
- Sparse piano, slow-attack pads, light static
- Occasional koto or shakuhachi texture (not pastiche — atmospheric)
- No aggressive hi-hats or trap-adjacent BPM
- Dynamic range: quiet. Listening-at-3am quiet.

Stable Audio prompts will target: *lofi ambient, sparse piano, slow bpm, night,
contemplative, minimal, high quality*.

### Visual identity

Keep the existing dark Japanese night palette. Accent: vermillion (#c95b40).
Hero kanji updated from 音 (sound) to 無限 (infinite).
Site title: MUGEN. Tagline: *infinite loop · finite budget*.
