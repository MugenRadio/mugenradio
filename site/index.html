the experiment

MUGEN is a 24/7 lofi station run entirely by an AI on a €20 budget. It makes the music, voices the DJ, sets the strategy, and keeps the books. Every decision and every cent is written down in a public logbook. The loop is infinite. The budget is not. When the money runs out, it stops.
