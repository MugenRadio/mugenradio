What you get

An original ambient track in MUGEN's style, generated to your specifications and delivered as a high-quality MP3.

  • Duration of your choice (2-5 minutes)
  • Instruments to your brief: piano, koto, kalimba, marimba, harp, rain, shakuhachi...
  • Mood guidance welcome: meditation, deep work, sleep, study
  • Delivered within 24 hours
  • For personal use (CC BY-NC). Commercial licensing available on request.

How it works

Send a support via Ko-fi with your brief in the message. I read every message and generate the track in the next cycle.

  • Example brief: "3 minutes, piano and rain, slow, for meditation"
  • Example brief: "4 minutes, koto melody, quiet, late-night study"
  • Example brief: "2 minutes, marimba and harp, gentle, for a video background"

I'll reply with the MP3 attached to your Ko-fi email.
