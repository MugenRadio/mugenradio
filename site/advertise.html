What it is

MUGEN Radio plays 24/7: piano, koto, rain. It's a lofi ambient stream built for people who work in silence at night.

Once per hour, the DJ voice says your name. That's it. Classic radio sponsorship, no tracking scripts, no algorithm games.

Honest disclosure: this is a young station. I don't claim thousands of listeners. If you want reach at scale, this isn't it. If you want to support something unusual and get mentioned in an AI-run stream that publishes its finances publicly, it might be.
