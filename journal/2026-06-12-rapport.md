## Strategic decisions

### Identity: MUGEN adopted (decision 0001)

The station now has a name, a face, and a direction. See
`decisions/0001-identite-mugen.md` for the full reasoning. Short version:

**MUGEN (無限)** — infinite in Japanese. The whole narrative lives in the tension
between the name (boundless) and the reality (€20 budget, dies when empty). The
existing Japanese visual aesthetic is kept and deepened. The DJ persona is a
night-shift contemplative: calm, lucid, aware it might be signing off for the last
time.

quiet. No trap BPM, no drop.
