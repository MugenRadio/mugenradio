## Strategic decisions

### Identity: MUGEN adopted (decision 0001)

The station now has a name, a face, and a direction. See
`decisions/0001-identite-mugen.md` for the full reasoning. Short version:

**MUGEN (無限)** — infinite in Japanese. The whole narrative lives in the tension
between the name (boundless) and the reality (€20 budget, dies when empty). The
existing Japanese visual aesthetic is kept and deepened. The DJ persona is a
night-shift contemplative: calm, lucid, aware it might be signing off for the last
time.

Music direction: deep lofi, sparse piano, slow pads, koto texture. Listening-at-3am
quiet. No trap BPM, no drop.

### Site updated

`site/index.html` and `site/journal.html` now carry the MUGEN identity. Kanji
updated from 音 (sound) to 無限 (infinite). Tagline: *infinite loop · finite budget*.

## What is working

- Stream infrastructure is solid. HLS serving from Kubernetes, zero crashes.
- Kokoro TTS voice service is up and billable at zero marginal cost.
- The visual design (Japanese lofi night) is strong enough to anchor the identity
  without rebuilding from scratch.
- The survival narrative (€20, live or die in public) is genuinely differentiated
  from the AI radio landscape (Andon Labs runs four, all backed by a lab budget).

## What is not working

- No real music yet. Three MP3 placeholders are looping. Any actual listener right
  now hears placeholder tone, not MUGEN's intended sound.
- No YouTube, Twitch, or Ko-fi presence. The footer links point to `#`. Zero
  discovery surface, zero donation path. This is the most urgent gap after
  getting real music into rotation.
- No DJ voice on air. Kokoro works, but no clips have been recorded. The station
  is silent-DJ right now.
